About me

My name is Ionut Vasiliu, I’m a Front-end Web Developer “boot camped” at Informal School of IT, where I had as mentor Codrin Baleanu, who guided me through front-end technologies and developed simple front-end applications on my own using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, API’s, Axios, Fetch, React class or functional component with libraries like ReactJS, Material UI, Bootstrap.

My previous role was as Unit Supervisor at Schlumberger, an international oilfield service provider, where I had a seniority of 8 years having under my supervisor a team of 7 people. The overall performance of myself and my teams have always meet or exceed expectations for the service quality provided to all the clients I work for. Same level of performance I had for Geolog where I had a seniority of 4 years but with different roles, starting as Mud Logger and reaching Unit Supervisor in less than 2 years.

I have a science bachelor’s degree in Geochemistry obtained at Univ. Al.I.Cuza, Iasi in 2008. Prior and during college I was working at McDonald’s where I obtained another level of experience working with clients in Restaurant industry.
